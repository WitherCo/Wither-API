Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wither API Gateway. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our API services and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data when you use our API Gateway:

  • Identity Data: username, email address, and other account details
  • Technical Data: API keys, IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location
  • Usage Data: information about how you use our API, including request logs and error data
  • Content Data: data you transmit through our API for processing

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our API services
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features when you choose to do so
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues
  • To fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, accessed, altered, or disclosed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know.

All API keys are hashed and stored securely. We never store your API keys in plain text.

5. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Request logs are retained for a maximum period of 30 days for debugging and troubleshooting purposes.

6. Your Legal Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
  • Right to withdraw consent

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7. Third-Party Services

Our API Gateway may integrate with or use third-party services. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our API Gateway.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
